PyAlgoTrade is Python library for backtesting stock trading strategies.

The two main goals are:

  • To make it easy to write and backtest a strategy with historical data.
  • To make it easy to scale horizontally, that is, using one or more computers to backtest a strategy. You can now take advantage of Google's cloud computing infrastructure and run your strategies inside Google App Engine.

The best way to get an idea of what is this about, is to take a look at the tutorial. The complete documentation is located here.
