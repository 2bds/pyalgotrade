PyAlgoTrade is a free and open source Python library for backtesting stock trading strategies.

The two main goals are:

  • To be able to easily write and backstest a strategy with historical data.
  • To make it easy to scale horizontally, that is, using one or more computers to backtest a strategy.

The best way to get an idea of what is this about, would be to take a look at the tutorial. The complete documentation is located here.
